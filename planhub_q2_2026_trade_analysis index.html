Definitions

Revenue range — self-reported via PlanHub integration, backfilled 7/12/26 (previously ZoomInfo). Coverage varies sharply by cohort: SC 59%, SP 4.9%, GC 2.8%. No way to distinguish which individual values are self-reported vs. legacy ZoomInfo.

Employee range — ZoomInfo-sourced. Coverage: SC 84%, SP 64%, GC 87%.

Company specialty (trade) — single-value, fed via Google Sheet integration. "Unknown" is a real category returned by the sourcing process, not a missing-data placeholder, and is reported separately from "Unspecified/Missing" (no specialty value at all).

Reg CVR vs. Deal CVR — Reg CVR = Closed Won ÷ Gross Registrations (full-funnel efficiency). Deal CVR = Closed Won ÷ Deals Created (sales-stage efficiency only, once a deal already exists).

Heatmap cells — cells with fewer than 15 companies show a sample count ("n=") instead of a CVR percentage, since conversion rates on very small samples are not reliable signals.
